# Partner Role

A partner is an organization or person that supports Fabric adoption, delivery, education, research, or ecosystem growth.

## Responsibilities

- make commitments explicit
- avoid community capture
- respect open governance
- disclose commercial interests where relevant
- contribute back when possible
- support users responsibly

## Privileges

- submit partner proposals
- participate in ecosystem planning
- publish public commitments
- collaborate on adoption programs

## Rule

Partner influence must be transparent and evidence-linked.
