# Contributor Role

A contributor is anyone who makes useful, respectful contributions to Fabric as Community.

## Responsibilities

- contribute clearly scoped work
- explain context and intent
- participate respectfully in review
- link relevant evidence
- support newcomers when possible

## Privileges

- open issues
- submit pull requests
- submit proposals
- participate in discussions

## Path to reviewer

A contributor may become a reviewer after repeated useful contributions, respectful collaboration, and demonstrated care for community quality.
