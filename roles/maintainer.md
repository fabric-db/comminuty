# Maintainer Role

A maintainer stewards a defined area of Fabric as Community.

## Responsibilities

- review contributions fairly
- merge only when quality and process are satisfied
- document important decisions
- protect contributor safety
- avoid conflicts of interest
- help grow future maintainers

## Privileges

- approve and merge changes
- moderate issues and discussions
- label and triage work
- participate in release and governance decisions

## Accountability

Maintainer privilege can be reduced or removed if it is misused or abandoned.
