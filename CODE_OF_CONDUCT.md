# Code of Conduct

Fabric as Community exists to help people build together with trust, respect, and transparency.

## Expected behavior

Participants are expected to:

- be respectful and patient
- welcome newcomers
- give constructive feedback
- credit others fairly
- focus on ideas, evidence, and impact
- respect privacy and consent
- avoid harassment, intimidation, and personal attacks

## Unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment or abuse
- threats or intimidation
- discriminatory language or conduct
- doxxing or privacy violations
- sustained disruption of community work
- bad-faith participation
- abuse of maintainer privileges

## Reporting

If you experience or witness harmful behavior, report it to the maintainers through a private channel when available.

Reports should be handled with care, confidentiality, and fairness.

## Enforcement

Possible outcomes include:

- reminder or warning
- mediation
- temporary limits on participation
- removal of privileges
- ban from community spaces

Enforcement decisions should be documented with appropriate privacy protections.
